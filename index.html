Methodology

Primary sources. No shortcuts.

Every dashboard is built from the actual filing or time series — not a vendor's pre-cleaned database. The arithmetic is the arithmetic an analyst would do manually, automated across decades.

SEC EDGAR XBRL, direct.

Structured financials pulled from the SEC's XBRL API. No Bloomberg, no consensus estimates, no "as-reported" database abstractions. The number on screen is the number in the filing.

Restatements preserved.

When a company restates (Plug's 2021 Amazon/Walmart warrant reclass, for example), both vintages are kept. The chart knows which year came from which filing — pre-restatement and post, side by side.

YTD → standalone, automated.

Quarterly cash flow series are reconstructed by differencing YTD 10-Q filings. 44 quarters of CFO / CFI / CFF, rebuilt the same way a human analyst would — but across every vintage, every ticker.

Footnotes surfaced inline.

Any dotted-underlined term shows the accounting definition on hover. Warrant contra-revenue, loss-contract provisions, DOE loan mechanics, ATM/SEPA dilution math — explained where they appear, not buried in a glossary.
