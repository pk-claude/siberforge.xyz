Methodology

  • Universe: 160 publicly traded names across 8 AI-beneficiary industries. S&P 500 members, >$10B mkt cap, or sector-thematic ETFs. No options. No leveraged/inverse ETFs.
  • Scenarios: 24-month directional return vs S&P 500. None = AI hype unwinds, hyperscaler capex normalizes. Modest = capex grows 15-20%/yr, gradual enterprise adoption. Strong = 25-35% capex growth, power binding constraint. Massive = AGI-adjacent shock, capex doubles.
  • Top-5 selection: composite score = confidence (1-5) × upside × operating margin under modest scenario.
  • Numbers vintage: historical figures from issuer 10-Ks; current quarter from latest earnings release; forward estimates are author projections under named scenario, not analyst consensus.
  • Not advice: framework for evaluation, not a buy list. Verify against current filings before any action.
Data: company 10-Ks/10-Qs (most recent reported), management guidance, public earnings press releases. Scenario probabilities are heuristic. Built 2026-04-25 · Not investment advice
