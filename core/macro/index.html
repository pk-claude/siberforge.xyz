Regime-Conditional Sector Returns

Each historical month is classified into one of four growth × inflation regimes. Cells show the average forward total return for SPY and each sector ETF given that regime. Growth = composite z-score of INDPRO, nonfarm payrolls, and real retail sales (each 6m annualized). Inflation = z-score of Core CPI 6m annualized. Both z-scores use a trailing 120-month window so the regime is “vs recent history” rather than against the inflationary 1970s.

Stock-bond correlation regime

The single most decision-relevant cross-asset signal for any 60/40-style portfolio. Below zero = bonds hedge equity drawdowns (the post-2000 default); above zero = bonds amplify equity drawdowns (the 1970s and 2022 regime). The flip determines whether duration is a diversifier or a correlated risk.

Data: FRED, Finnhub. SPY proxies the S&P 500. ISM PMI substituted with Industrial Production (ISM data is not licensed to FRED).
