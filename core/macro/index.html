Stock-bond correlation regime

The single most decision-relevant cross-asset signal for any 60/40-style portfolio. Below zero = bonds hedge equity drawdowns (the post-2000 default); above zero = bonds amplify equity drawdowns (the 1970s and 2022 regime). The flip determines whether duration is a diversifier or a correlated risk.
